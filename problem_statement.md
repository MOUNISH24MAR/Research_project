# Problem Statement

## AI-Powered Smart Interview Preparation System

Interview preparation is a major challenge for students and job seekers seeking employment opportunities. 
Many candidates possess the required technical knowledge but struggle to perform well in interviews due to inadequate preparation, lack of confidence, poor communication skills, and limited exposure to real interview environments.

Current preparation methods, such as practicing from question banks, watching tutorials, or attending mock interviews, often fail to provide personalized feedback and accurate assessment of a candidate's strengths and weaknesses.
Additionally, candidates rarely receive company-specific interview practice tailored to the organizations they are targeting.

Another significant challenge is the absence of a comprehensive system that can simultaneously evaluate technical knowledge, communication skills, answer quality, and candidate confidence. 
As a result, many candidates are unable to identify areas requiring improvement before attending actual placement or recruitment interviews.

Therefore, there is a need for an intelligent interview preparation platform that can simulate real interview experiences, assess candidate performance, provide personalized feedback, and help users improve their interview readiness.
Such a system should enable candidates to practice effectively, track their progress, and enhance their chances of success in placement and recruitment processes.
