Problem Statement

Many students struggle to prepare for interviews due to:

Lack of personalized feedback
Difficulty identifying weak areas
Limited interview practice opportunities
Lack of confidence during interviews
Proposed Solution

The proposed system provides an AI-powered interview platform that:

Analyzes resumes and calculates ATS scores
Conducts technical and HR interviews
Evaluates candidate answers using AI
Detects emotions during interviews
Generates feedback and performance reports
Provides company-specific interview preparation
Tracks candidate progress over multiple sessions
Objectives
Improve interview preparation efficiency
Provide realistic interview experience
Identify candidate strengths and weaknesses
Increase confidence through continuous practice
Enable targeted company-specific preparation
